OCCY - Who stole the supper??

The youth group supper has gone missing. Three suspects — Jan the Janitor, Sally the Snack Lady, and Tim the Tech Guy are under suspicion.

How to play

  1. Three teams, each team must have a leader with a smart phone.
  2. You will solve the mystery by finding clues hidden around the church building. Each clue will lead you to the next clue, there are 30 clues, you must find the clues in order to solve the puzzle.
  3. Each clue is in the form of a QR code, when you find the QR code scan it on your phone to get the next clue.
  4. In addition to finding the clue to the next QR code, you will also see some letters in a blue box on each clue. Take a note of those letters as you go as they will help you solve the mystery.
  5. The first team to solve the mystery will be rewarded.

The letters on each clue should end up saying:

Jan didn’t do it. Tim didn’t either. Sally definitely did.

These QR codes will be hidden around the church.
